My Information

Through the completion of a Full-Stack Web Development bootcamp offered by UNCC from April 20, 2021 through October 14, 2021, I have had the privilege to learn many intricate details revolving around website creation and working as part of an effective team. Throughout the course of my bootcamp experience, I was able to work with many different groups made up of different ideas and ways of thinking. Our grades were comprised primarily of 3 group projects which involved planning, designing, and building a website of our choosing around specific guidelines. During these assignments, I took pride in my ability to communicate effectively, lead our group through adversity and challenges, and deliver a quality product for each project. While much of the project was completed individually, faculty encouraged us to communicate with each other to ensure quality teamwork. On the off chance we were not able to find information through given bootcamp material, we were encouraged to think on our feet and search for answers while utilizing outside resources. These experiences have given me a refined ability to implement complex guidelines to exact specifications given.


These are the languages I learned during my bootcamp; MySQL, Node, Javascript, React, Bootstrap, HTML, CSS, Mongo, Express, Heroku, Jest, Jquery, Npm, Sequelize and Handlebars.
